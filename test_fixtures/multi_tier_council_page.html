Community Grants Programme 2024-25

Supporting local community projects and initiatives

Small Grants (up to £5,000)

For smaller community projects and initiatives.

Funding Details

Grants of up to £5,000 available for projects that benefit the local community.

Application Window

Applications open from 1st September 2024 and close on 30th November 2024 at 5:00 PM.

Project Delivery Period

Projects must be completed between 1st January 2025 and 31st March 2025.

How to Apply

Complete the online application form for small grants.

Apply for Small Grant

Large Grants (over £5,000)

For larger community projects and strategic initiatives.

Funding Details

Grants from £5,001 to £25,000 available for significant community projects.

Application Rounds

Applications are accepted in two rounds:

  • Round 1: Opens in November 2024, closes in January 2025
  • Round 2: Opens in April 2025, closes in June 2025

Project Delivery Period

Projects must be completed between 1st April 2025 and 31st December 2025.

How to Apply

Complete the comprehensive application form for large grants.

Apply for Large Grant

General Information

All grants are subject to availability and successful applications.

For more information, contact the grants team at grants@example-council.gov.uk
